After taking a critical look at my life and seeing how the fear of speaking in public had prevented and was still preventing me from reaching my sales and financial goals, experiencing a significant growth in my career and personal life, working in the industry that was aligned with my life’s purpose, I made the decision, and believed without a doubt in my mind, that I was going to overcome the fear of speaking in public no matter what it would take.

I began to search for uncommon ideas and strategies on how to overcome the fear of speaking in public. I then started to experiment with the different ideas and strategies that I discovered through research and observation.

After I had tried out different ideas and strategies, I realized that there were several things that you could do to overcome the fear of speaking in public. But I also discovered that out of these things that you could do that there were 3 of them that you could do without having to go through the stress of leaving your home or standing in front of a live audience to practice.

Here are 3 things:

1. Believe

You have to believe that these strategies would work for you. You have to believe in your ability to do the work required. You have to believe in the value that you have to offer.

2. Master

You have to master your content. You have to master your presentation style. And you have to master your voice.

3. Share

You have to share your content on social media – on your timeline, in groups, and with certain individuals.

When I decided that I was going to become a soft skills training facilitator, I wasn’t sure how I was going to achieve it because I was still struggling with the fear of speaking in public. But after I applied these 3 strategies, it became a lot easier for me to speak in front of a live audience.

After about 6 months of doing these 3 things, I got my first major speaking engagement where I trained over a hundred entrepreneurs on customer service, and what amazed me was that it didn’t feel like my first time of training that number of people.

I was also invited to speak on 2 online shows, got a contract to create 13 online audio/visual courses for a human capital development organization, and I also got a training contract with a public speaking academy.

I have a client who also applied these strategies and after about 3 months she was able to host 3 online workshops and 1 webinar, and also spoke on an online show as a quest speaker.
